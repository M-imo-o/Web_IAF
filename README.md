# InAmigos Foundation Awareness Webpage

A modern and responsive NGO awareness webpage created for InAmigos Foundation (IAF) to showcase its impactful social initiatives, ongoing projects, and mission to create positive change in society.

## About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a youth-driven NGO focused on social welfare, education, environmental protection, animal care, women empowerment, and skill development. The organization works through various impactful initiatives including:

* **Seva** – Supporting people in need with food, clothes, and essentials
* **Bachpanshala** – Providing education and care for underprivileged children
* **Jeev** – Animal rescue and welfare initiatives
* **Udaan** – Empowering women through skills and opportunities
* **Prakriti** – Environmental awareness, clean-up drives, and plantation programs
* **Vikas** – Internship and youth skill development programs

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to spread awareness about the NGO’s mission and initiatives through a clean, user-friendly, and visually engaging webpage.
