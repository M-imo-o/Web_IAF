Who We Are

About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered, Central Government licensed non-profit organisation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), headquartered in Chhattisgarh.

Driven by a commitment to empowering lives through social service, we unite passionate volunteers across India to deliver meaningful change — from education and animal welfare to environmental conservation and community development.

Holding 80G & 12A certifications, we ensure full transparency, accountability, and tax-exempt benefits for donors. Our CSR-1 registration enables us to partner with corporates for high-impact CSR initiatives, while our NITI Aayog registration aligns our mission with national development goals. We are also proud holders of the IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification — a mark of our unwavering commitment to quality in operations and service delivery.
